Slyvon Blanco is an independent web consultant, strategist, designer, and chicken wings addict. When he's not talking about himself in 3rd person, he's usually spending most of his time making websites for clients or consulting with them about how they can improve what they currently have.

Anyway — in all seriousness — I wear a lot of hats, but here are some of the things I do:

* Develop sales funnels (or optimizing what already exists to improve conversions)
* Create evergreen email marketing campaigns
* Plan and execute product launches
* Make systems and processes to streamline operations
* Or simply provide direction with big picture stuff

As a freelance consultant, I've had the privilege of working with other entrepeneurs and taking part in many exciting projects. If you're interested in working together, you can learn more about my services and view some of my work [here](https://design.slyvon.com).

## Me IRL

I'm a minimalist. I love anything that has a clean, aesthetically-pleasing design.

I have a thing for simplifying complexity. Because why make things harder than they need to be?

Home base is in Illinois. But in 2015, I gave away everything I owned and traveled the world. My whole life was in one bag, and I lived out of AirBnbs. *(Fall 2019 update: I'm currently living in the San Diego area.)*

I gave up social media in the middle of 2017. I still have my accounts, but they're practically dormant. If I ever decide to use them again, you can follow me on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/slyvonblanco/) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/slyvonblanco).

In my free time, I make things like [SimplifiedFitness.io](https://simplifiedfitness.io/).

I also try to [write stuff](/blog) every now and then. If you're curious to know how I work, check out my [Tools](/tools) page for a list of my favorite products & services.
