I'm Slyvon Blanco (most people just call me Sly), and I help small business owners stand out and make more money in the digital world.

I wear a lot of hats, but I spend most of my time making websites for clients or consulting with them about how they can improve what they currently have.

Some of the things I do include:

* Developing sales funnels (or optimizing what already exists)
* Creating evergreen email marketing campaigns
* Planning and executing product launches
* Making systems and processes to streamline operations
* Or simply providing direction with big picture stuff

As a freelance consultant, I've had the privilege of working with other entrepeneurs and taking part in many exciting projects. If you're interested in working together, you can learn more about my services and view some of my work [on this page](https://design.slyvon.com)

## Me IRL

I'm a minimalist. I love anything that has a clean, aesthetically-pleasing design.

I have a thing for simplifying complexity. Because why make things harder than they need to be?

Home base is in Illinois. But in 2015, I gave away everything I owned and traveled the world. My whole life was in one bag, and I lived out of AirBnbs. *(Update: I'm currently living in the San Diego area.)*

When I'm not in front of my computer, you can probably find me at a coffee shop drinking a flat white... at a gym lifting heavy things... or at a restaurant eating chicken wings. 

I gave up social media in the middle of 2017. I still have my accounts, but they're practically dormant. If I ever decide to use them again, you can follow me on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/slyvonblanco/) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/slyvonblanco).

In my free time, I make things like [SimplifiedFitness.io](https://simplifiedfitness.io/).

I also try to [write stuff](/blog) every now and then. If you're curious to know how I work, check out my [Tools](/tools) page for a list of my favorite products & services.
